About us

Soulsist is a online marketplace that connects users to local brands and boutiques based on location and interest.
This allowing the same day delivery, focusing on user satisfaction and allowing small and medium brands improve their engagement by incresieng their online presence.
We also include national delivery, to give brands a wider range of costumers.

Philosophy

We strive to support local brands by giving them a space where they can exhibit their products with soul and grow.
Our goal is to make shopping local easier by helping people have better acces to a large collection of independent brands.
